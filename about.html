Our Story

From the streets of Gaborone to the world

Philosophy & Craftsmanship

Stimela's Street Couture was born from a vision to bridge the gap between high fashion and authentic street culture.

Every piece is designed with the urban explorer in mind — durable, stylish, and unapologetically bold. We work with local artisans and source sustainable materials to create streetwear that not only looks good but does good. Our mission is to put African street fashion on the global map.

Our Journey

2026

Stimela's Street Couture founded in Gaborone, Botswana with a vision to revolutionize African streetwear.

2027

First flagship store opens. Debut collection "Genesis" sells out within weeks.

2028 & Beyond

Expanding across Africa while staying true to our roots — authentic, bold, and for the culture.
